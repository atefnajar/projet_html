To amuse himself, Ryuk tosses his Death Note into the human world. Light stumbles upon it and initially scoffs at the first rule: whoever's name is written in this journal will perish. However, he is ultimately tempted and decides to test the Death Note by writing the name of a criminal, which leads to their unexpected and violent death.
