The plot of Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters portrays the journey of the ancient Egyptian pharaoh's spirit as he sets out to uncover his forgotten identity. Yugi Mutou, the individual responsible for awakening the pharaoh's spirit, teams up with his comrades to engage in multiple skirmishes against individuals linked to the pharaoh's past.
