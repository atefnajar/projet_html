In the midst of fierce competition among schools for soccer supremacy in Japan, Raimon Middle School's soccer club, Inazuma Eleven, faces the threat of being disbanded. Mamoru Endou, the team's captain and goalkeeper's grandson, accepts the challenge of reinvigorating the struggling team. Though aware of the difficulties ahead, he remains optimistic that with a bit of luck and some help, he can turn things around.
