Throughout his entire existence, Makunouchi Ippo has endured endless bullying, leading him to yearn for change. However, despite his desire, he lacked the bravery to pursue his aspirations. That is, until the infamous boxer Takamura Mamoru rescues him from an attack and brings him to a nearby gym for recovery.
