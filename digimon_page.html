Ten-year-old Taichi Yagami is gearing up for his summer camp when peculiar happenings begin to occur in Tokyo - several electronic systems start to malfunction. Upon realizing that his sister and mother are stuck on an unstoppable train, Taichi rushes to the nearby station. Shockingly, he gets transported to an alternate world where he encounters a peculiar creature named Agumon, who already knows his identity. Taichi also obtains a peculiar gadget called a "Digivice" that lets him interact with the digital world.
