With aspirations of becoming a successful soccer player, Tsubasa and his mother moved to Nankatsu city in hopes of joining a top-tier elementary school soccer team. However, Tsubasa quickly realized that the competition in Nankatsu was far more challenging than what he faced in his hometown, and he had to push himself harder than ever before to stand out.
