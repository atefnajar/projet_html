Accompanied by his faithful Pikachu, Satoshi embarks on a mission to become a "Pokémon Master," joined by Takeshi, a trainer who adores female Pokémon, and Kasumi, a robust trainer who holds an affection for Satoshi. The trio journeys across different regions, including Kanto, the Orange Islands, and Johto, battling tough competition at the Pokémon League.
